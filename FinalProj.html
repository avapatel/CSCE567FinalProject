Introduction

The Washington Commanders have been in a rut for quite some time. That was until Heisman Trophy recipient Jayden Daniels signed to the Washington Commanders. This project explores key stats comparing the Washington Commanders pre and post Jayden Daniels.

Daniels VS Howell: Simple Stats

Daniels (24) and Howell (23) both played and started in 17 games in their respective seasons. Jayden Daniels attempted a total of 480 passes and completed 331 where Howell attempted a whopping 612 passes and only completed 388 of them. During the 2024-2025 regular season, Daniels had a passing success rate of about 47% and gained on average 10.8 yards per pass. During the 2023-2024 regular season, Daniels had a passing success rate of about 42% and gained on average 10.2 yards per pass. Sam Howell was sacked 65 times where Daniels was sacked only 47 times. In both aspects Daniels overall is better when it comes to not throwing interceptions and passing touchdowns. Howell threw 21 interceptions where Daniels only threw 9. When it came to passing touchdowns, Daniels threw 25 where Howell threw 21.
